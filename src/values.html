What is NEWFTEC?

NEWFTEC is a technology management consulting company that aims to bring the scale, capability, and security of enterprise-grade technology to the small and medium business market to give your customers and employees the experiences they demand. We focus on quick wins aligned to strategic priorities and well architected patterns, focus on building capabilities that you own and can scale with a broad developer base.
We design and deliver solutions that are:

Powerful

Technology solutions that enable real competitive differentiation

Opportunity is endless for businesses today that want to tap into big data, social media, IoT, geospatial data and more. Your company's solutions should be up to the task and your technology footprint should facilitate options, not introduce constraints.

Agile

Technology scale on your terms - flexibility is key

Technology is a powerful, yet fickle force. Strength comes from flexibility, decoupling, and a light touch that allows you to scale at your pace. No matter your current position, there are ways to get started today to improve your competitve position and the value to your customers.

Durable

Outpaced solutions that last

Technology solutions rely upon strong foundations that are stable, supported, and known to scale. Newftec solutions are designed with cloud-native, well architected principles, enterprise best practices, and long-term supportability in mind, so that you can focus on strategy and execution.

Secure & Protected

Security and monitoring central to design

Newftec allows you to take advantage of cloud monitoring tools and security processes that the world's best enterprises rely upon. Leveraging these tools, you can own, configure, and monitor your security, giving you and your customers verifiable piece of mind.

Dedicated

Our business is building your trust and capabilities

Newftec is about providing you solutions that you own and configure - vs selling you the latest software solutions - built on enterprise cloud architecture patterns that fuel the world's top innovations. We are dedicated to empowering you to achieve more.
